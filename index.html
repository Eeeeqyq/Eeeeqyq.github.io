Welcome to My Website

I’m a second-year data science student at Northeastern University with interests in applied machine learning, health informatics, and scientific computing. I enjoy turning raw data into clear stories and practical tools. In coursework and projects, I’ve worked across Python, R, and web technologies to build small but useful things: from interactive visualizations to reproducible analytics pipelines. I’m especially drawn to projects where I can explore the full stack of the data workflow—collecting or sourcing datasets, cleaning and organizing them, designing visual encodings, and communicating insights in a way that is accessible to non-technical audiences.

Outside class, I’m developing my research skills through an independent transcriptomics project that analyzes immune responses to COVID-19. This work has taught me how to read papers critically, compare methods, and keep careful records so results are reproducible. It’s also made me appreciate the collaboration between domain experts and data scientists. As I grow, I aim to contribute to teams that value clarity, integrity, and learning. I’m currently looking for opportunities—co-ops, internships, or collaborations—where I can apply my skills, learn from mentors, and deliver measurable value.
