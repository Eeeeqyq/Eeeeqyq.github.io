About Me

I'm a sophomore studying Data Science at Northeastern University (Minor in Mechanical Engineering, 3.2 GPA). My work sits at the intersection of applied ML, data engineering, and software development — I enjoy taking a problem from raw data all the way to a working, interpretable system.

Recent projects include an end-to-end ML pipeline predicting student debt payoff timelines across 6,300+ U.S. institutions, and a goal-conditioned ambient music recommendation agent that fuses wearable signals with multi-source audio features. Before university, I spent nearly three years as an Operations Associate at TYB, automating trading reports with Python and shipping the company's web presence.

I'm actively seeking a co-op or internship from May to December 2026. Outside of work you'll find me in the gym, tinkering with drones, following motorsport, or deep in a video game.
